How to Play

Aim & Shoot: Move mouse/touch to aim, hold to fire

Place Towers: Click tower buttons or press 1-4

Tower Types:

  • Basic - Balanced (100g)
  • Sniper - Long range (200g)
  • Splash - Area damage (175g)
  • Slow - Slows enemies (125g)

Upgrade/Sell: Click a tower, then U to upgrade or S to sell

Pause: Press Space
